Compose

Constructor

new Compose(lensA, lensB) → {Compose}

Create the composite of two `lens`es. For instance, to create a lens for arr[i][j]: arrIJLens = new Compose( new IndexedLens(1), new IndexedLens(0) ); Then arrIJLens.get, .set, and .over all work as you'd expect.
